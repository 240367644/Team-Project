About Us


Our aim is to support university students who are moving into an accommodation for the first time (or returning) by providing high-quality, affordable essentials.

We understand that moving can be overwhelming, trying to design your own space while also keeping it comfortable, so we're here to lighten the load! With fast delivery and reliable customer support, we will make your move in stress-free.
